Philosophy

THERE IS NOTHING COOLER THAN HOLDING SOMETHING YOU MADE.

I'm an industrial designer driven by one obsession: the moment an object lands in your hand and just feels right. That's not luck — it's geometry, material, and intention working together invisibly.

I work in Rhino 3D and Keyshot, building forms that are as rigorous under the hood as they look on the surface. My Mini Clubman project taught me that a closed mesh matters more than perfect detail — real objects have constraints.

Currently studying Industrial Design, pushing every project toward something that could actually be made, held, and used.
