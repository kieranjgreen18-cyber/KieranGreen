About

Obsessed
with the
feel of
things.

I'm an industrial designer driven by one obsession: the moment an object lands in your hand and just feels right. That's not accident — it's geometry, material, and weight working together invisibly.

My Mini Clubman project taught me that a closed mesh matters more than perfect detail. Real objects have constraints, and working within them is where the interesting design happens.

Currently studying Industrial Design — pushing every project toward something that could actually be made, held, and used.
