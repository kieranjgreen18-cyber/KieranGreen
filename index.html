About Kieran Green

Kieran Green is an industrial designer specialising in CAD modeling, surface development, FDM prototyping, and product visualization. Seeking internships, freelance projects, and collaborations.

Skills and Software

  • Rhino 3D — surface modeling, SubD modeling, CAD modeling
  • Keyshot — product visualization and rendering
  • Premiere Pro — motion and animation direction
  • FDM Prototyping — physical model production
  • Form Development — ideation through manufacture-ready geometry
